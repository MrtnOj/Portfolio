DiscHub

Disc golf scoring application designed for easy use on mobile devices

Description

Primary function of this application is to offer a quick and simple way to mark scores while playing a round of disc golf. The app has very basic authentication and for logged in users, the rounds are saved and displayed on nifty scorecards. There's also a feature for checking weather forecast for the location of courses because nobody wants to play in the rain.

  • node.js
  • express
  • React.js
  • MongoDb Atlas
  • Mongoose ORM
